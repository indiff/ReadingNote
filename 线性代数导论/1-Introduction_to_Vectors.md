# Introduction to Vectors

## Vectors and Linear Combinations

**VECTOR ADDITION** $
v =
\begin{bmatrix}
v_1 \\
v_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$ and $
w =
\begin{bmatrix}
w_1 \\
w_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$ add to $
v + w = \begin{bmatrix}
v_1 + w_1 \\
v_2 + w_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$

**SCALAR MULTIPLICATION** $
2v =
\begin{bmatrix}
2v_1 \\
2v_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$

The sum of $c\boldsymbol{v}$ and $d\boldsymbol{w}$ is a linear combination of $\boldsymbol{v}$ and $\boldsymbol{w}$
