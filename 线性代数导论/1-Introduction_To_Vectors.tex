\section{Introduction to Vectors}

\textbf{VECTOR ADDITION} $
v =
\begin{bmatrix}
v_1 \\
v_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$ and $
w =
\begin{bmatrix}
w_1 \\
w_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$ add to $
v + w = \begin{bmatrix}
v_1 + w_1 \\
v_2 + w_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$

\textbf{SCALAR MULTIPLICATION} $
2v =
\begin{bmatrix}
2v_1 \\
2v_2 \\
\end{bmatrix}
$

The sum of $c\textit{\textbf{v}}$ and $d\textit{\textbf{w}}$ is a linear combination of $\textit{\textbf{v}}$ and $\textit{\textbf{w}}$
